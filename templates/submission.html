üìä Detailed Submission Requirements

Paper Format

  • IEEE Conference format mandatory
  • Maximum 6 pages including references
  • PDF format only
  • Font: Times New Roman, 10pt
  • Two-column format with single spacing
üîç

Review Process

  • Double-blind peer review
  • Minimum 3 reviewers per paper
  • Reviews based on originality, technical quality, and clarity
  • Plagiarism check using advanced tools
  • Author feedback incorporated
üë§

Author Guidelines

  • Papers must be in English
  • Original work not published elsewhere
  • At least one author must register
  • Author names and affiliations removed for review
  • Maximum 8 authors per paper
